iSpring Suite

iSpring Suite is an all-in-one e-learning authoring toolkit that allows users to create interactive online courses, quizzes, video lectures, and role-play simulations directly within Microsoft PowerPoint. It functions as a PowerPoint add-in, requiring virtually no coding or design experience to use.
