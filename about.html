My Story

I began my career in the late 1990s as a Corporate Training Specialist, quickly advancing from a new-hire trainer to a software and technology trainer. Over the past two decades, my professional journey has spanned multiple disciplines, including Information Technology and Software Applications, Insurance Licensing, Sales and Customer Service, Leadership, and Ethics.

Early in my career, I recognized that effective training extends far beyond delivering content. That realization led me to further my education in Content Development, Instructional Design, Web Development, and Teaching in Higher Education. These experiences have shaped my strategic, learner-centered, and innovation-driven approach to instructional design.

Since 2017, I have served in Design and Development at Bellevue University as an Instructional Designer. In this role, I integrate evidence-based design principles and emerging technologies to create engaging, high-impact courses that enhance the student learning experience and support measurable academic outcomes.


AI Integration and Emerging Technologies

I actively incorporate Artificial Intelligence and emerging technologies into both course design and development workflows. I leverage generative AI platforms such as ChatGPT to assist faculty with curriculum planning, refining learning objectives, developing assessments and rubrics, and creating adaptive and applied learning activities.

In addition, I guide faculty in the responsible and ethical integration of AI into coursework. This includes designing AI-informed assignments, critical thinking exercises, and workplace-aligned applications that prepare students for technology-driven environments. My approach emphasizes thoughtful prompt engineering, iterative refinement, and alignment with established instructional frameworks such as ADDIE and Bloom’s Taxonomy. I view AI as a strategic enhancement tool—one that strengthens sound pedagogy rather than replacing it.


Professional Philosophy

My vision for instructional design centers on innovation, purposeful technology integration, and strong collaboration with Program Directors and faculty partners. By working closely with subject matter experts, I strive to push the boundaries of what is possible with each course build—balancing academic rigor, creativity, and meaningful learner engagement.

Outside of my professional work, I enjoy spending time with my family and playing the guitar.
